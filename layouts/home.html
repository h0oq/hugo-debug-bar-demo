The home page uses layouts/_default/baseof.html as a wrapper and layouts/index.html for its content block.

# copy baseof.html into layouts/_default/
# copy index.html into layouts/
hugo server

Open http://localhost:1313 to see your demo site, then customize the HTML sections.
